About Me

Hello! My name is Dianne Eramo and I live in Baltimore, MD. In my spare time I do a partnered social dance called Lindy Hop, cook food from around the world, go on runs with my dogs, play my ukulele, and travel around the country and world whenever I get the chance.

My degree is in economics and my work background is in financial operations, administration, and accounting, with a foray into owning my own women’s hair accessories business. Well, that’s a wide range, you may think, and admittedly it is. I went into finance, as many do, to seek financial security, which was my primary motivator until relatively recently. I quit my accounting job in September 2015 to take the front end development course at Betamore and haven't looked back. I’m looking forward to a career in which passion, and not fear, is my primary motivator.
